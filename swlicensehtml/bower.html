AnchorLinkInstalling CppLicen.se

CppLicen.se is an online dashboard for you to track your licenses, and an open-source C++ SDK for your Windows application.

  • Login on CppLicen.se using your Google account
  • Download the C++ SDK using git: git clone https://github.com/li0nel/cpplicense
  • Personalise your SDK with your own application_id and application_secret
  • In your software, call bool SWLicense::Licensing::CheckLicenseValid() on startup to authenticate your users.
  • From CppLicen.se dashboard, start adding users for your software by entering their email, the license start date, and end date
  • Your customers receive their license by email with instructions to login to your software.
  • You keep traces of your software activations, use, and eventually abuses. If needed, revoke any license at any time.

A note about offline use

You decide how much time your customer can use your software offline. However you'll have finer tracking and control over your licenses when enforcing your customers to regularly connect their machine to internet to periodically login to re-activate their licenses.
CppLicense SDK uses a silent mode to re-activate licenses, prompting users for credentials only about once a year.
